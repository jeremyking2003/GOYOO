Customer Service Department

Customer Service Center is responsible for all the company's pre-sale, sale, after-sales service. Customer service center to establish a sound service system, and by a strong, well-trained, strong technical service team, the greatest degree of assurance to provide customers with strong material security.

Customer service center with product, engineering services, the Ministry of Commerce three functional departments. Product Department is mainly responsible for product support technical advice, engineering services for the global customer product installation and maintenance, the Ministry of Commerce is mainly responsible for receiving customer complaints, customer relationship maintenance. Engineering Services consists of more than a dozen skilled and experienced technical support engineers, responsible for the global technical support work, centralized acceptance of customer service requests, fault reports and customer complaints.
